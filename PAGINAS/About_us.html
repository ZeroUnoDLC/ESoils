About us

ESoils is a university project that aims to make soil data recording accessible through an efficient and effective application to anyone who needs to record soil data. Whether you are a researcher, a student, a farmer, or just curious about soil issues, ESoils can help you collect, store and manage your soil data with ease.

We are Anthony, Rafael and Brayan, software students who developed this website as part of our learning journey. We started with small projects that made us think about how to solve problems by applying logic, sometimes in games like 8 queens, magic square, among others, until we became familiar with more complex projects like this one.

We believe that soil data are important for understanding the environment, agriculture and climate change. We also believe that everyone should have access to this data and be able to use it for their own purposes. That's why we created ESoils, an easy-to-use and reliable website that allows you to record and manage your soil data easily and conveniently.
